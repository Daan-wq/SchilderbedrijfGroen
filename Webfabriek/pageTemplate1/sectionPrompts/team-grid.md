## Structure
- **Container**: Full-width section with generous padding.
- **Layout**: 3-column symmetric grid (`grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3`).
- **Gap**: Moderate gap (`gap-6` to `gap-8`).

## Grid Item (Team Card)
- **Shape**: Vertical rectangle or square, rounded corners.
- **Background**: Solid color (pastel or brand accent) as backdrop for person.

## Card Content
1. **Person Image**: Cut-out/transparent background image of person, positioned at bottom of colored backdrop.
2. **Text Block** (below or overlaid at bottom):
   - Name: Bold, medium size.
   - Role/Title: Smaller, subtle color.

## Styling Options
- Each card may have a unique background color from a palette.
- Images are consistently sized/cropped.

## Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop (md+)**: 3-column grid.
- **Tablet (sm-md)**: 2-column grid or horizontal scroll.
- **Mobile (<sm)**: 1-column stack or carousel.
