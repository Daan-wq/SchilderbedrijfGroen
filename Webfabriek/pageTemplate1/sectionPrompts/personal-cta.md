## Structure
- **Container**: Full-width section with generous padding.
- **Layout**: 2-column symmetric grid (`grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2`, 50/50 split).

## Left Column (Text)
- Contains:
  - Section Title (H2).
  - Body paragraph (personal, inviting tone).
  - CTA button (primary style).
- Alignment: Left, vertically centered.

## Right Column (Image)
- **Media**: Portrait photograph of a person.
- **Shape**: May have organic/rounded shape, or rounded rectangle.
- **Fit**: `object-cover`, person is the focal point.

## Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop (md+)**: Side-by-side columns.
- **Mobile (<md)**: Stack vertically (Text above Image, or Image above Text).
