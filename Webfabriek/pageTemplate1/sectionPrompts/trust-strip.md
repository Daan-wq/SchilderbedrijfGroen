## Structure
- **Container**: Full-width, slim section.
- **Height**: Compact (`py-6` to `py-10`).
- **Background**: Matches page background or very subtle contrast.

## Internal Layout
- **Flex Row**: `flex`, `justify-center` or `justify-between`, `items-center`, `flex-wrap`.
- **Gap**: Generous horizontal spacing between logos (`gap-8` to `gap-12`).

## Logo Styling
- **Color**: Monochrome (grayscale or single muted color).
- **Opacity**: May have reduced opacity (e.g., `opacity-50` to `opacity-70`).
- **Size**: Consistent max-height constraint across all logos.

## Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop**: Single row, all logos visible.
- **Mobile**: May wrap to 2 rows or reduce number of visible logos.
